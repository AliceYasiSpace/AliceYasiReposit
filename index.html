The project "Mapping Chicago's Lost Chinatown (MCLC)" aims to present the first Chicago's Chinese neighborhood located in the Loop, intersected by Van Buren, Clark, and Harrison Streets. Undertaking the method of geographic ethnography, it uses maps to re-exhibit the life of the immigrants first coming to Chicago before their relocation to the current Wentworth Chinatown. The maps will combine photos, immigration profiles, and city directories to visualize the community living of the Chinese Americans in Chicago from the 1880s to 1920s.
